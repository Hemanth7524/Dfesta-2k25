Welcome to the Data Science Department Fest! We are passionate about data, innovation, and creativity!

Dr. Kusuma

Head of the Department

Dr. Kusuma, the Head of the Department of Data Science, is a visionary leader with extensive experience in the fields of machine learning, artificial intelligence, and big data analytics.
With a strong academic background and numerous research publications, she has been instrumental in shaping the curriculum and fostering a culture of innovation within the department.
Her leadership ensures that students and faculty stay ahead in the rapidly evolving world of data science.
She actively mentors students, collaborates on industry projects, and plays a key role in organizing the Data Science Fest, providing a platform for knowledge sharing and technological advancements.

Mr. Kiran Kumar

Assistant Professor

Mr. Kiran *Assistant Professor – Data Science & Head Faculty,DEFESTA Mr. Kiran is a passionate educator and researcher in the field of Data Science, specializing in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics.
As an Assistant Professor, he is dedicated to delivering high-quality education, mentoring students, and driving research initiatives in emerging technologies. In addition to his academic role, Mr. Kiran serves as the Head Faculty of DEFESTA, where he plays a pivotal role in organizing and leading data science festivals, workshops, and industry collaborations.
His leadership fosters innovation, encouraging students to engage in real-world problem-solving through hackathons and research projects. With a commitment to academic excellence and practical learning, he continues to shape the future of aspiring data scientists. 🚀
